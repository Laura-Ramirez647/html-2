Chocolate Chips Cookis

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps to prepare

  1. Pheheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, add to batter along with salt.
  4. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  5. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  6. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Optional toppings or variations

  • Top with a sprinkle of sea salt before baking for a sweet and salty twist.
  • Substitute white chocolate chips or dark chocolate chunks for a different flavor.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon or espresso powder to the dough for extra depth.
  • Drizzle melted chocolate over cooled cookies for a more decadent treat.
